Take out the heavy lifting from your site processes
Ensure only approved contractors or visitors enter your site after completing the relevant workflow. Ideal for creating inductions, permits, SWMS, access requests, and inspections.
Set up single or multiple workflows for your sites, and have workflows completed before, during, and after checking in to a site.
Company Inductions made easy
Let Sine handle the hassle of getting inductions completed before a contractor arrives on site.
Automate the process by setting up site-specific or national inductions and have contractors complete them from the Sine Pro mobile app or web dashboard. Include videos, images, or document upload.
Prescreen visitors with COVID-19 questions
Use Sine Workflows to prescreen your visitors before they check in by preregistering their details and sending them an email invite.
Gain key COVID-19 information from your visitors with check-in information and QR code to scan on arrival. Put access requests in a ‘pending’ state and approve or reject on a case-by-case basis or automatically.
Easy Workflow setup
Flexible settings allow you to set up and share a workflow for any scenario share within minutes. Build customised questionnaires, forms, and required information, and integrate with sites. Adjustable validity periods mean you can set any expiry time or ask the contractor to choose.
Share workflows
Easily copy a magic link from the Workflows dashboard to share via email or text. Contractors and visitors who receive the notification can self register and get pre-approved. Once the workflow is completed, they are then able to check in to the site.
Mobile Friendly
Optimised for busy contractors to complete on the move
Review & filter
Monitor a workflow and view by status type and site. Searchable name field allows you to easily find the person you are looking for. Track responses with pending approval, and dive deeper to see a respondents form answers and document uploads.
